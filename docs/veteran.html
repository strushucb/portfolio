Veteran Status

I served as either an enlisted member or a commissioned officer of the United States Army, the Army National Guard, and the Army Reserve for 13 years. My service is highlighted by three tours to Afghanistan where, leading both American & Afghan communication and intelligence operations, I worked with the local population and security forces to counter the ongoing insurgency.
